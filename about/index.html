Our Mission*

Memo* is an art and design magazine which features a dozen mediums of art all made by a group of small artists. The magazine is made to be a showcase of young and aspiring creatives who deserve more recognition for the professional standards of art they create. The nature of the internet magazine allows for even more categories of media to be displayed than is possible by a print magazine. Video, animation, computer science, no creative left behind.

The Format*

Memo* is not a print magazine, but an interactive website formatted like a magazine. Some pages are just web responsive equivalents of normal magazine pages with text and photographs, but most will also contain interactive elements, videos, links and other things no print magazine could have. Accessible from anywhere the internet reaches, Memo* is the only magazine you could see from space.

Release Timeline*

Issue 1 is currently in the works. The artists are being gathered and the magazine is coming together. The end of 2021 is the target release time, however the timeline is still very vague.
